The Two-Day
International Conference

It is indeed our privilege to be the hosts for the ‘International Conference on Neo Era in Forensic Science’ to be held on 22nd and 23rd April, 2023 at Galgotias University, Greater Noida. The Conference has been designed for professionals engaged in various fields of Criminal Investigation, research and academics. The aim of the conference is to provide an opportunity to bring together the scientific community involved in the various aspects of criminal investigation, as well as to share and discuss the various advances in this field. This two-day International Conference will have lectures and paper/poster presentations by the participants representing various Forensic Science institutions/organizations.We take this opportunity that is worth in every aspect endorsing the knowledge and to hone skills to keep us updated in the area of Forensic Science. It will be our endeavor to make this learning experience an academic feast for one and all.

ABOUT US

About the University:

The Galgotias University NAAC A+ Accredited, Uttar Pradesh, sponsored by Smt. Shakuntala Educational and Welfare Society has been established by the state of Uttar Pradesh under section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956 vide The Galgotias University Uttar Pradesh Act, 14 of 2011. The University commenced its operation during the academic session 2011-12. Galgotias University opened its door to the first batch of approximately 1,700 undergraduate and postgraduate students in July of 2011. Now in the year 2023, the University has grown to more than 25,000 students

About the School:

School of Basic & Applied Sciences is one of the founding schools of Galgotias University. It comprises of the following divisions - Forensic Science, Physics, Chemistry, Biochemistry, and Clinical Science. It is home to 108 nationally and internationally recognized faculty members of which 85% of the members are doctorates from national institutions like IIT, NIT, DU & BHU. Most faculty members in the school have industrial experience and balance their efforts between teaching and applied research.

About the Division:

The Division of Forensic Science at Galgotias University offers B.Sc. (H), M.Sc., and Ph.D. programs in Forensic Science. GU had taken the initiative to start the aforesaid programs of Forensic Science from the academic year 2012 with the objective to impart students with a strong theoretical, practical, and experimental foundation and thereby gain specialized skills that would enable them to execute forensic expertise. Currently, we have 300 plus students in graduation and post-graduation programs and more than 50 Ph.D. scholars pursuing Forensic Science. The Division has fully equipped Labs and well-maintained infrastructure. The Division regularly arranges student and expert interactions, workshops, seminars, webinars, guest lectures, and conferences.
